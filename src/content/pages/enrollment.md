Join the Milestones family. We process all enrollments securely through our online Family Portal system.

## New Families
If you are new to Milestones, you must first create an account and submit your initial registration information.  
[Start New Registration](https://www.schoolcareworks.com/reg_milestones/start_registration.jsp)

## Existing Families
Current families should use the Family Portal to register for upcoming sessions, manage tuition, and update information.  
[Log Into Family Portal](https://connect.schoolcareworks.com/milestones_login.jsp)
