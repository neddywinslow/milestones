At Milestones, we recognize that our teachers are our most valuable resource. We strive to create a collaborative, supportive, and professional atmosphere where educators can grow in their careers.

## Why Work With Us?

- **Professional Environment:** Work alongside experienced educators who are leaders in the Early Childhood and School Age fields.
- **Impact:** Make a meaningful difference in the lives of children and families in the Milwaukee area.
- **Growth:** We support ongoing professional development and training.

## Current Openings

We are currently accepting applications for Teachers, Assistant Teachers, and Camp Counselors with open availability.

Please send your resume and a cover letter to: [info@milestonesprograms.org](mailto:info@milestonesprograms.org)
