Milestones strives to keep our programs accessible while maintaining the highest quality of care and education for your children.

Tuition rates vary depending on the specific program, age of the child, and schedule selected (full-time vs. part-time, before or after school, etc.).

### Current Rate Information
To receive the most accurate and up-to-date tuition schedules for your family's needs, please contact our administrative office.  
[Contact Us for Rates](/contact)

## Financial Assistance
We believe that every child deserves a great start. Milestones accepts Wisconsin Shares child care subsidy payments. If you need assistance navigating that process, our administrative team is happy to help provide the necessary provider numbers.

## Payment Information
For enrolled families, all tuition statements, invoices, and payments are managed securely through the [Family Portal](https://connect.schoolcareworks.com/milestones_login.jsp). We offer convenient options for automatic withdrawal and online payments.
