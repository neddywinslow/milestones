Milestones' Early Childhood program provides nurturing and play-based learning. We understand that early experiences shape a lifetime of learning, which is why our educators act as partners to parents in these crucial early years.

## Philosophy
Our integrated curriculum is tailored to every stage of a child's development, ensuring a balance of directed activities and creative expression.

## Environment
Our safe, modern facilities are specifically designed for infants and toddlers, featuring stimulating toys and a clean, warm environment.
