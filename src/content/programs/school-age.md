Milestones' School Age program is designed with the unique needs of older children in mind. Operating out of our Lydell Community Center and Indian Hill School locations, we provide a structured yet flexible environment where students can thrive outside of regular school hours.

## Before & After School
Mornings start calmly, allowing children to prepare for the school day ahead. In the afternoons, children can unwind, participate in recreational activities, and get a head start on their homework. 

### Homework Support
Our staff is available to assist students with their assignments, providing a quiet space and the guidance needed to foster good study habits.

### Social Development
We emphasize character building, cooperation, and conflict resolution through team games and community projects.
